Hello and welcome to our ASL (American Sign Language) Learning Zone!

Here, children and families can learn how to talk using their hands — a fun and beautiful way to communicate! This section is specially made for children with hearing or speech challenges, and also for anyone who wants to learn to connect with others in a new way. You’ll find easy-to-follow hand signs, friendly pictures, and step-by-step guides to help your child spell words, express feelings, and say their name using ASL.
