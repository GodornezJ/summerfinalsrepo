Now, let’s talk about the other letters in the alphabet — They are called Consonants!

Consonant letters help us start and end many words. Try saying these:

  • B is for Ball ⚽
  • M is for Monkey 🐒
  • S is for Sun ☀️
  • T is for Tiger 🐯
  • Z is for Zebra 🦓

They work together with vowels to make words sound right! Without consonants, we’d only have vowel sounds like “a-a-e-e-o-o”
